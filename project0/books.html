My Book Recommendations

  1. Wings of Fire, by Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam



    2. APJ Abdul Kalam is an author, scientist, and former President of India. It's his story from a newspaper boy in chilhood to a Scientist and then the President of India.


  2. Everyday Greatness, by Stephen R. Covey



    3. Inspiring stories and practical insights challenge readers to live a life of everyday greatness. Best-selling author Stephen Covey and Reader’s Digest have joined forces to produce an extraordinary volume of inspiration, insight, and motivation to live a life of character and contribution. The timeless principles and practical wisdom along with a "Go-Forward Plan" challenge readers to make important choices every day.


  3. The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People, by Stephen R. Covey



    4. One of the most inspiring and impactful books ever written, The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People has captivated readers for 25 years. It has transformed the lives of presidents and CEOs, educators and parents—in short, millions of people of all ages and occupations across the world. This twenty-fifth anniversary edition of Stephen Covey’s cherished classic commemorates his timeless wisdom, and encourages us to live a life of great and enduring purpose.
